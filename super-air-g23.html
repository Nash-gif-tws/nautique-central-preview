Overview

The undisputed boat of choice.

For over a decade the Super Air Nautique G23 has been the most sought-after wake boat on the water, and the flagship of the G-Series. Its meticulously refined hull throws clean, consistent and fully customisable wakeboard wakes and surf waves at the touch of a screen.

It scales from a relaxed first ride to the kind of pro-level wake that redefines what's possible, which is why serious riders and families alike keep coming back to it. On the water it feels composed and purposeful, with a cabin built entirely around board sports.

Best for Serious surf and wake families who want the industry benchmark.

What sets the G23 apart.

Nautique Surf System

NSS with WAVEPLATE technology shapes a scalable, switchable surf wave on either side instantly.

Configurable Running Surface

NCRS extends from the hull to fine-tune wake and wave shape to each rider's preference.

Custom Ballast

Up to 3,600 lbs of sub-floor ballast sculpts the wake without sacks cluttering the cockpit.

Fast-Fill Ballast

Reworked plumbing fills the ballast system around 40% faster between sets.

LINC Panoray

A 15-inch helm touchscreen centralises wake settings, speed, audio and boat systems.

Flight Control Tower

Offered in manual, actuated or telescoping form to clear low garages and bridges.

JL Audio M6

An M6 Series Elite stereo package comes standard for full-volume sound across the boat.

Integrated Steering Assist

Standard assist with optional Auto Steer makes docking and rider pickups effortless.
