Overview

Push your limits, not your budget.

The S240 is the big boat you didn't know you could afford: 24 feet of EZ-V Hull that seats 16 and throws long, pushy waves with a level ride that keeps the driver's sightlines clear. It's a genuinely easy boat to learn behind, building your surfing one wave at a time.

Up to 4,800 pounds of ballast and the standard Fast Mover Ballast System fill up to 68% faster, so the wait for fun is almost nothing. Add the QuickSurf System for waves that rival far pricier tow boats. Premium presence, practical price.

Best for Larger crews and growing families who want pro-level surf and room to spare.

Book an on-water demo

What sets the Supreme S240 apart.

EZ-V Hull

Modified deep-V hull with 20 degrees of deadrise delivers long, pushy waves and a level, visible ride.

Fast Mover Ballast

Standard 13.5-gallon-per-minute SeaFlo pumps fill up to 4,800 pounds of ballast as much as 68% faster.

QuickSurf System

Available surf system shapes waves that rival premium tow boats and switches sides on demand.

Silent Stinger Wake Plate

Optional Silent Stinger and Stinger Wake Plate refine wave height and shape on the water.

Supreme Omni Dash

Touchscreen dash centralises ballast, surf and stereo control at the helm.

Wet Sounds Audio

Champ Wet Sounds speakers and a dash stereo remote keep the energy up all day.

Comfort-Strong Seating

A spacious lounge with plush Comfort-Strong upholstery seats up to 16 across the cockpit.

Smart Storage

Hinged lounge seating opens to Rhino-Lined storage that keeps gear from sliding around.

Every detail considered.
