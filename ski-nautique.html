Overview

An icon, evolved.

The Ski Nautique is the evolution of a true icon, purpose-built for three-event waterskiing across slalom, trick and jump. Significantly lighter than its predecessor thanks to a carbon-fibre and fibreglass hull, it produces naturally flatter wakes with more support through the trough.

It carries more personal bests and world records than any waterski boat in history, making it the choice of dedicated skiers and clubs. On the water it rewards technique, holding a clean line and consistent speed pass after pass.

Best for Dedicated slalom and three-event skiers chasing personal bests.

Highlights

What sets the Ski Nautique apart.

01

Carbon-Fibre Hull

Carbon fibre, fibreglass and an integrated stringer system balance strength against minimal weight.

02

Automated HydroGate

Fine-tunes the running surface automatically across ten customisable positions for a flatter wake.

03

MicroTuners

Patented surfaces disrupt water flow to fill the wake troughs for cleaner crossings.

04

Integrated Zero Off

GPS-based speed control holds consistent speed through every pass of the course.

05

LINC Panoray

The touchscreen automates HydroGate and MicroTuners based on the discipline you select.

06

Trick Ballast

An optional trick ballast configuration has set the stage for numerous trick world records.

07

Ski Cradles

Purpose-built open cradles lined with NauTeak Non-Skid stow skis within easy reach.

08

Convertible Seating

Removable three-cushion rear seating converts into a coaching platform when needed.
