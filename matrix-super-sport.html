Overview

Tournament wakes, all-rounder value.

The Matrix Super Sport is the all-rounder of the range, built in Australia from fibreglass and aimed at families and skiers who want serious performance without a flagship price. At around 21 feet it's light, easy to tow and quick to launch, yet still carries the crew with larger observer seating and a big rear lounge.

It's tuned for the water first: precise tracking, sharp handling and pillow-soft, tournament-style wakes that suit serious slalom skiers. For buyers chasing a genuine ski boat that also doubles as a capable family day-boat, it covers the bases at a sensible price.

Best for Skiers and families wanting a true tournament-style ski boat that's an affordable, easy-to-tow all-rounder.
