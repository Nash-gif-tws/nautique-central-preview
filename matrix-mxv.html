Overview

From barefoot to surf.

The MXV is the newest boat in the Matrix range and a true crossover, built to handle everything from barefoot to surf. Designed and built in Australia from fibreglass, it's compact on the outside but roomy inside, with seating for up to 10 and a clean, USA-style interior as standard.

Its edge is efficiency. Where oversized imports drink fuel, the MXV keeps running costs realistic without giving up performance. Fibreglass floors and engine bearers, double pump-in-and-out ballast and an MTX folding tower come standard, with factory upgrades available to tailor it to how you ride.

Best for Buyers who want one efficient, do-everything tow boat spanning barefoot, ski and surf.
