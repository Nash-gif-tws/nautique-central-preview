Overview

Unlimited possibilities.

The Super Air Nautique GS22 is the most popular multi-sport boat on the market, and it's easy to see why. Its hull blends the running surfaces of the record-setting Ski Nautique and the award-winning G-Series, so one boat genuinely covers slalom, wakeboard and surf.

At 22 feet it's an approachable, versatile package that opens the door to almost any sport behind it. Families who can't choose a single discipline get clean ski wakes, crisp wakeboard wakes and a proper surf wave from the same hull.

Best for Multi-sport families who want one boat to do everything.

Highlights

What sets the GS22 apart.

01

Multi-Sport Hull

Combines Ski Nautique and G-Series running surfaces for genuine slalom, wakeboard and surf ability.

02

Nautique Surf System

NSS creates a clean, switchable surf wave at the touch of a button.

03

Configurable Running Surface

NCRS extends from the hull and deploys automatically to refine wake shape.

04

Supplemental Ballast

Two 250 lb sacks lift total ballast to around 2,900 lbs for bigger waves.

05

Ski Pylon

A dedicated tow point delivers a low, true pull for slalom and trick skiing.

06

LINC Panoray

An intuitive 12.4-inch touchscreen controls wake settings, speed and onboard systems.

07

Flight Control Tower

Available with an actuated option, carrying M6 tower speakers with integrated down lighting.

08

JL Audio M3

An M3 Series stereo comes standard, with M6 tower-speaker upgrades on offer.
