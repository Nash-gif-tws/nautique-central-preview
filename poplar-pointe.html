The family that
lives on this lake.

Most dealers sell you a boat and wave goodbye from the parking lot. Kevin and Shawnna Graham bought this dealership in 2020 and moved the whole family to the lake — Cooper runs the shop, Colson sells the boats, and the marina, the Shady Shack Grill and the Surf Garage all sit on the same point.

A full-service marina

Buy it, slip it, service it and fuel it in one place. Your boat lives where the people who sold it work every day.

A family that answers

Call the shop and you get a Graham, not a call center. Straight answers, factory parts, done when we say.

The whole lake day, one point

Demo a boat, grab lunch at the Shady Shack Grill, pick up a surf setup at the Lake Gaston Surf Garage. Make a day of it.
