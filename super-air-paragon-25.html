Overview

Luxury for everyone.

The Super Air Nautique G25 Paragon is the larger flagship of the Paragon range, built on Nautique's award-winning G-Series hull and styled for those who refuse to choose between performance and refinement. It generates world-class wakes and waves while seating the whole family in genuine luxury.

Paragon-exclusive touches include dual 15-inch LINC Panoray displays, a telescoping Flight Control Tower, meticulously crafted upholstery and finishes, and a full JL Audio M6 Studio Elite system. An available Yanmar 8LV diesel completes the most spacious, luxurious G-Series Nautique on the water.

Best for Larger families and entertainers wanting flagship performance with the most room and luxury.

Highlights

What sets the G25 Paragon apart.

01

Dual LINC Panoray

Twin side-by-side 15-inch touchscreens, found only on Paragon models, give effortless command of every system.

02

Telescoping Tower

The Flight Control Tower telescopes up or down at the touch of a button and projects the Nautique logo at night.

03

Diesel Option

An available Yanmar 8LV diesel produces 370 hp and 595 ft-lb of torque for relaxed, efficient cruising.

04

Nautique Surf System

NSS with WAVEPLATE forms customisable waves on either side with no ballast shifting required.

05

Configurable Surface

The Nautique Configurable Running Surface auto-deploys to fine-tune wake shape and boat attitude.

06

Supplemental Ballast

An optional 1,200 lb of subfloor ballast offsets smaller crews so the wave stays full-size.

07

JL Audio M6

A complete JL Audio M6 Studio Elite system fills the cockpit with sound, with transom-ported WakeSub subwoofers.

08

Luxury Seating

Convertible transom seats with flip-up tray tables and a reversible stern seat maximise space and comfort.
